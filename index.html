The game is simple, you need to gamble. There will be questions on the side, for you to

answer, the value of the variable or simplified fractions will be the chances of you winning

so you can choose accordingly. IF you get the correct answer for the answer of the question,

it will give you a hint on getting the right answer. Still though, its pretty hard.

After you failed, or won, press retry to go again.

PS: For the hints, you might get a negative number or a number thats not in the choices for the

ranges, for example, -1 < n < 13. With this, don't worry, your choice is still within the selection given to you.

Also, the * sign is representing multiplication.


You need to press the number buttons, according to the number that you are guessing.
